WHO AM I?

My name is Elijah Appelson, and I am a Mathematician and Activist based in the Catskill Mountains in rural New York. I graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Vassar College with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Statistics. Currently, I serve as the Data Analyst at the ACLU of Louisiana, where I work to advance police and prison accountability and safeguard civil liberties by amplifying lived experiences and making data accessible to all stakeholders. Outside work, I am interested in graph theory, data privacy law, jewelry making, boxing, and reading.
